Coaching Services

Private Sessions: One-on-one technical coaching for paddlers of all levels, focusing on stroke technique, course navigation, and race tactics.

About Nick Collier

Nick is a U23 Canoe Slalom World Champion and silver medalist in Kayak Cross. With years of coaching experience at club and national levels, he combines elite athletic insight with practical training to help paddlers reach their best.

Passionate about whitewater sports, Nick emphasises safe, effective, and enjoyable coaching for all ages and abilities.
